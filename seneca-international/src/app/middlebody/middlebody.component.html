About Seneca – NIFM Partnership

Seneca and NIFM partnership is to work together on ongoing short-term training to NIFM participants, in the field of public sector financial management. The customized training is designed on contemporary financial management topics such as Fin-Tech: Innovation in Finance, Cybercrime, Anti Money Laundering, Fraud Detection and Prevention and Bitcoin and Blockchain. The training program is delivered at Seneca in association with Seneca’s School of Accounting and Financial Services. For past two years, over 150 Government of India Officers have received training at Seneca.

About NIFM

National Institute of Financial Management (NIFM) was set up in 1993 as an autonomous body under the Ministry of Finance, Government of India. NIFM is registered as a society.

The Union Finance Minister is the President of the NIFM Society. The Institute, created as an apex training institution for centralized and uniform training of Civil Services Probationers. Recruited through the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) of India for Central Finance & Accounts Services), represents the training mandate for the following services:

  • Indian Audit & Accounts Service
  • Indian Civil Accounts Service
  • Indian Cost Accounts Service
  • Indian Defense Accounts Service
  • Indian P&T (Finance & Accounts) Service and
  • Indian Railway Accounts Service of Government of India.
